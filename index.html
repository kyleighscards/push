How to Play Push!

Goal: Be the first to get rid of all your cards!

Setup

Each player gets 26 cards. There are 3 piles in the middle where you play cards.

Taking Turns

Players strictly alternate turns. Your card is drawn automatically - click a pile to play it.

Special Cards (J, Q, K, A)

Special cards start a countdown. A total of this many number cards must be played:

  • Ace: 4 number cards
  • King: 3 number cards
  • Queen: 2 number cards
  • Jack: 1 number card

When someone plays the final number card, the OTHER player takes the pile!

Special Rules

  1. Special card on special card = YOU take the pile (except Jack on Jack)
  2. Jack on Jack = the OTHER player takes the pile!
  3. A new special card on a number card starts a new count
  4. After ANY play, it becomes the other player's turn

You Win!
